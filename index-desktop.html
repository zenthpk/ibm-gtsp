Programs


Kaiwa Lounge

Beginner-friendly • June 20 - August 29 (11 weeks)

Kaiwa Lounge is a beginner-friendly program for people to meet new friends and practice group conversation exchange. Join us on Saturdays from 7-8 PM PDT to practice speaking Japanese and English with Japanese students and community members.

Kaiwa Partners

Intermediate & Advanced • Session A: July 5 - August 1 (4 weeks), Session B: August 2 - August 29 (4 weeks)

Kaiwa Partners is a program for intermediate-advanced speakers that matches participants up with an exchange partner for weekly conversations in English and Japanese. Build on previous conversations with your partner and develop your language abilities!

Frequently Asked Questions


What is KaiwaLounge?

KaiwaLounge is an online language exchange community for students. Through our university partner network, we host different programs throughout the year to help foster language and cultural exchange between participants from different backgrounds.

Who can join?

We invite participants to join our program through our partner universities, currently limited to the University of California, Middlebury College, ICU, and Hosei University. If your university is not listed - please reach out to us by email if you're interested in partnering with us!

How can I get involved?

If you were invited by an existing participant/coordinator or are a student at one of our partner universities, you can sign up directly for our programs. This Summer, we're hosting Kaiwa Lounge and Kaiwa Partners. Please check program descriptions for more information.

How can I become a Coordinator?

Coordinators help make sure our programs run smoothly. Please reach out to us by email if you're interested in being a Coordinator. Coordinators with at least intermediate proficiency in Japanese and English are preferred.
